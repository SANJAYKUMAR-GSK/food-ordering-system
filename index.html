About Taste Hunt

Since 2005


Welcome to Taste Hunt, where flavor meets passion. Located in the heart of Coimbatore, our cozy ambiance and expertly crafted menu make every meal special. From quick bites to leisurely dinners, we promise a memorable dining experience.

Savor fresh, locally-sourced ingredients and innovative dishes that delight the senses. At Taste Hunt, every visit is a celebration of good food and great company. Come dine with us and make each moment delicious.

Our Menu

Chettinad Biryani

Fragrant basmati rice infused with a rich blend of Chettinad spices, layered with tender marinated meat, and slow-cooked to perfection. Served with a side of cooling raita and crispy pappadum.

Thalappakatti Biryani

Aromatic basmati rice cooked with succulent pieces of marinated meat, infused with a secret blend of traditional Thalappakatti spices. Served with refreshing raita and a crisp, golden pappadum.

Mandi Biryani

Fragrant basmati rice cooked with tender, spiced meat, gently simmered to infuse every grain with rich, smoky flavors. Garnished with toasted nuts and fresh herbs, and served with a side of creamy yogurt sauce and a tangy salad.

Hyderabadi Biryani

Aromatic basmati rice layered with succulent marinated meat, slow-cooked to perfection with a blend of exquisite spices and saffron. Topped with crispy onions and fresh herbs, and served with a side of cooling raita and spicy salad.
